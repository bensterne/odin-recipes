Gnocchi Recipe

Description

I fell in love with gnocchi as a boy in the Italian restaurant in my small town in New York. This ricotta gnocchi recipe was passed down to me by my great-aunt. It is easy, traditional, and delicious!.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 2 large eggs
  • Â½ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour, or as needed
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 (15.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 6 basil leaves, finely shredded

Steps

  1. Prepare the gnocchi: Stir together ricotta, eggs, Parmesan, salt, pepper, and garlic powder in a large bowl until evenly combined.
  2. Divide dough into 3 or 4 pieces and roll into 1/2-inch thick ropes on a floured surface.
  3. Make the sauce: Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir in garlic and cook until softened and fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. While sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil over high heat. Boil the gnocchi until they float to the surface, 1 to 2 minutes, then drain.
  5. Stir cubed mozzarella into the sauce and allow the heat of the sauce to soften but not melt the cheese.
